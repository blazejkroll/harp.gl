intersectMapObjects

  • intersectMapObjects(x: number, y: number): PickResult[]
    • Defined in harp-mapview/lib/PickHandler.ts:134

    Does a raycast on all objects in the scene; useful for picking. This function is Limited to objects that THREE.js can raycast. However, any solid lines that have their geometry in the shader cannot be tested for intersection.

    Parameters

    • x: number

      The X position in CSS/client coordinates, without the applied display ratio.

      The Y position in CSS/client coordinates, without the applied display ratio.
